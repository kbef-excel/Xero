# Xero Bookkeeping Simulation — KBF Financial Services

A hands-on bookkeeping project completed in Xero (cloud accounting software), simulating a full accounts receivable cycle for a fictional Ghanaian financial services company.

---

## Company Setup

- **Company Name:** KBF Financial Services
- **Location:** Ghana
- **Platform:** Xero (free trial — cloud-based)
- Configured company profile, invoice template, payment instructions, and branding.

---

## What Was Done

### 1. Service Item Creation
- Created item code `CONSULT001`: *Bookkeeping and Financial Reporting Service*
- Assigned price, revenue account, and item description in Xero's inventory/items module.

### 2. Customer Invoice
- **Customer:** ABC Trading Ltd
- **Amount:** GHS 2,500
- **Payment Terms:** 14 days
- Invoice was raised, reviewed, and approved — officially recorded in the accounts receivable ledger.

### 3. Payment Recording
- Marked the invoice as paid in Xero
- Payment linked to the bank account, updating the accounts receivable balance automatically.

### 4. Bank Reconciliation
- Matched the customer payment to the corresponding bank transaction
- Confirmed books are accurate and reconciled.

---

## Skills Demonstrated

- Setting up a company and chart of accounts in Xero
- Creating service items for invoicing
- Generating and approving professional invoices
- Recording customer payments (accounts receivable)
- Performing bank reconciliation
